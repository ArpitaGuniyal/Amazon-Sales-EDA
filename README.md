# 🛒 Amazon Sales Analysis - Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

## 📌 Project Overview

This project performs **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)** on an Amazon Sales dataset to uncover valuable insights into product pricing, discounts, customer ratings, reviews, and product categories. The objective is to understand customer purchasing behavior and identify trends that can support better business decisions.

The project demonstrates the complete EDA workflow, including data cleaning, preprocessing, visualization, and business interpretation using Python.

---

## 🎯 Business Objective

The objective of this project is to analyze Amazon product data to identify meaningful insights related to:

- Product pricing strategies
- Discount patterns
- Customer ratings and reviews
- Product category performance
- Customer engagement

The findings can help businesses optimize pricing, improve promotional strategies, and enhance customer satisfaction.

---

## 📂 Dataset Information

- **Dataset:** Amazon Sales Dataset
- **Records:** 1,465
- **Features:** 16

The dataset includes information such as:

- Product Name
- Product Category
- Actual Price
- Discounted Price
- Discount Percentage
- Customer Rating
- Rating Count
- Product Reviews
- Product Links

## 📈 Key Insights

- Most products receive customer ratings above **4.0**.
- A few product categories dominate the dataset.
- Higher discounts do not necessarily result in better customer ratings.
- Actual Price and Discounted Price show a strong positive relationship.
- Customer engagement varies significantly across products.
- Premium products require different pricing and marketing strategies than budget products.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

- Focus on maintaining product quality to sustain high ratings.
- Implement targeted discount strategies instead of excessive discounts.
- Invest in high-performing product categories.
- Encourage customer reviews to improve product visibility.
- Differentiate marketing strategies for premium and budget products.
